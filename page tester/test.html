    About

    Disney On Ice presents Into the Magic is an ice skating extravaganza that features many of Disneys brightest stars and beloved songs. Hosted by Mickey Mouse, Minnie Mouse, Donald Duck and Goofy, Disney On Ice presents Into the Magic showcases world-class ice skaters and dazzling special effects. The show reimagines the stories of Coco, Tangled and Cinderella and features performances of classic Disney songs such as Be Our Guest from Beauty and the Beast, Moana's Grammy-winning How Far I'll Go, Frozens worldwide hit Let It Go and more.

    Disney On Ice is intended for a general audience. All guests ages two and up are required to have a ticket. Children under two are admitted free provided they sit on a parent or guardians lap. Some arenas may require all guests, regardless of age, to have a ticket. Please check with your local venue for exact details. 

    Disney On Ice events are casual and family-oriented. Guests may dress as their favorite character but not pose for pictures or sign autographs for other guests. Costumes and costume masks may not be worn by guests 14 years and up.

    For a detailed breakdown of attire recommendations and rules for Disney On Ice, please visit the Disney on Ice FAQ.
