NBA Tickets 202324

After the NBA Preseason kicks off in early October, the NBA regular season will tip off on October 24, 2023. There will be a break for All-Star Weekend, leading up to the NBA All-Star Game on Sunday, February 18, 2024, at Gainbridge Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, Indiana. Once the season resumes, it will be a race to the finish culminating in the NBA Play-In Tournament and NBA Playoffs, which are scheduled to begin in April 2024. Be sure to add your team to your favorites on Ticketmaster.com or in the Ticketmaster app to be the first to know when tickets are available.

NBA Playoffs

The NBA Playoffs will be preceded by the Play-In Tournament, where the 7th through 10th ranked teams in both conferences compete for their spot in the playoffs. The NBA Playoffs will then take place starting in April 2024 and running until the NBA Finals begin in June 2024. Theyll feature the top eight teams from each conference competing in a best-of-seven series as they look to advance to the following rounds.

NBA Finals

The NBA Playoffs reach their apex each year with the NBA Finals a winner-takes-all, best-of-seven series between the top teams from the Western and Eastern Conferences. In the 2023 NBA Finals, the Denver Nuggets beat the Miami Heat in a hard-fought five-game series to capture their first Larry OBrien Championship Trophy.
