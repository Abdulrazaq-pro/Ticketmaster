FAQ

See our complete list of FAQs for this upcoming MLB Season.



  • How much do MLB tickets cost?

    The team determines the original price for an MLB ticket, which can fluctuate by game. Pricing for resale tickets meaning those sold by fans, season ticket holders, and professional resellers is determined by the seller and can differ from the original ticket price.

  • When does the 2024 MLB season start?

    Opening Day for the 2024 MLB regular season is March 28. All 30 teams are scheduled to play on Opening Day. Before the regular season officially kicks off, teams participate in Spring Training games, which this year goes from February 22 through March 26.

  • When can I buy tickets for the 2024 MLB season?

    Tickets for the 2024 MLB season are now available at Ticketmaster. As the worlds largest ticket marketplace, Ticketmaster has a wide selection of seats available at competitive prices so you can be there live to cheer on your favorite MLB team all season long.

  • How many games are in the 2024 MLB season?

    Each MLB team will play a total of 162 games from spring to fall during the 2024 MLB regular season, with a brief pause for the All-Star break.

  • Are there any changes to the MLB schedule this year?

    For the 2024 MLB season, every team will match up against every other team at some point during the regular season. Each team will play 52 games against division opponents. Additionally, each team will play 64 interleague games (32 at home and 32 away). A total of 46 interleague games will be played by each team (American League vs. National League and vice versa).

  • How do I access my MLB tickets?

    All MLB tickets purchased on Ticketmaster are mobile. This means that your phone is your ticket. You can access your mobile MLB tickets in My Account on Ticketmaster.com or in the Ticketmaster app. If you purchased resale tickets, and Ticketmaster is not the original ticketer, the tickets will be transferred directly to you from a trusted seller. Ticketmaster will email you with instructions on how to accept the tickets on the original ticket providers mobile app.

  • What is the MLB Ticket Exchange?

    The MLB Ticket Exchange is Ticketmasters safe and secure marketplace for purchasing tickets to see your favorite MLB team live. You can find standard tickets for select MLB teams, including resale tickets sold by fans, season ticket holders and professional resellers. Ticketmaster gives you a safe and convenient way to buy or sell tickets for the 2024 MLB season.

  • Is the MLB playing any games outside of the USA this season?

    Major League Baseball will host several games outside of the United States in 2024. For the last 30 years, MLB has played more than 200 international games in 11 different countries and territories.

    The 2024 MLB World Tour: Mexico Series will take place April 2728, 2024 in Mexico City, Mexico, featuring a two-game series between the Houston Astros and the Colorado Rockies. This will mark Major League Baseballs second consecutive trip to the Mexican capital.

    From June 89, the New York Mets and the Philadelphia Phillies will face off at London Stadium for the MLB World Tour: 2024.

Additional Ticket Information

MLB Regular Season Ticket Information

Spring training begins in February and marks the start of the MLB Regular Season when teams set their rosters for Opening Day. This year, youll have 162 opportunities to see your favorite team from the stands thats how many regular season games each team will play in the 2024 season. Check the MLB Schedule to find out when your favorite team is in town and mark your calendar with the games you dont want to miss.

There is a break in the regular season for the MLB All-Star Game, which will be hosted this year on July 16 at Globe Life Field in Arlington, Texas. The MLB All-Star Game brings together the best players in the league, and features a whole week of events. Be sure to grab your tickets for this special game.

 

MLB Postseason Ticket Information

When the regular season ends on September 29, the top 12 teams from the American and National Leagues enter the MLB Postseason, and hopefully your team will be one of them.

The MLB postseason hosts 12 teams and should begin on October 1 with six Wild Card teams facing off against two division winners. It then shifts to the Division Series where the winners of the Wild Card winners Series and the two highest-seed Division winners face off. The victors advance to the League Championship Series to go head-to-head for the Pennant and a spot in the World Series. You wont want to miss this truly one-of-a-kind experience, so be sure to grab your MLB Postseason Tickets when they become available.

 

MLB World Series Ticket Information

The 2024 MLB World Series begins a few days after the ALCS and NLCS winners are determined. Being there live for the World Series is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness the two best teams competing in a best of seven series for the ultimate prize in baseball. At the 2023 World Series, the Texas Rangers beat the Arizona Diamondbacks 5-0 in game five to secure their first- ever World Series title. Learn more about the previous World Series winners.
