    About

    P!NK is one of pop musics most dynamic vocalists and stage performers. Since releasing her debut studio album Can't Take Me Home in 2000, the three-time Grammy Award-winning artist (born Alecia Beth Moore) has sold 60 million albums worldwide, with four No. 1 songs on the Billboard Hot 100 and three No. 1 albums on the Billboard 200. Between 2001's quintuple-platinum M!ssundaztood and 2003's Try This, which featured the Grammy-winning single Trouble, P!NKs raspy, rock-influenced vocals became the bedrock of the modern pop rock genre. P!NK also cemented her status as a touring powerhouse when she wrapped 2018-19s Beautiful Trauma World Tour, which became the 10th highest-grossing tour ever. P!NKs concert tours often incorporate stunning gravity-defying performances, and at the 2010 Grammy Awards, she performed Glitter in the Air on aerial silks high above the crowd delivering one of the most memorable performances in Grammy history.
