    About

    Shania Twain is the best-selling female artist in country music history, with five Grammy Awards to her name and an expansive career that continues to raise the bar. The Canadian singer-songwriters third studio album, 1997s Come On Over, is one of the best-selling albums of all time; with over 40 million copies sold worldwide, it produced smash hits such as You're Still the One, That Don't Impress Me Much and Man! I Feel Like a Woman! In 2022, Twain released the Netflix documentary Not Just A Girl, which highlights her legendary career as the Queen of Country Pop and the catalyst of the country-pop crossover of the 90s. Twain has also sold out arenas on multiple world tours, including 2015s Rock This Country Tour, which grossed $65 million across North America, according to figures reported to Billboard Boxscore. In 2023, she embarked on the Queen of Me Tour, her first tour in nearly five years, in support of her sixth studio album Queen of Me.
